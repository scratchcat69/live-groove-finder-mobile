## Description (4000 chars max)
Hear a song. Find the show. Get tickets.

Live Groove Finder is the ultimate companion for live music fans. Recognize songs playing live, discover concerts near you, and build your personal music history — all from one app.

DISCOVER MUSIC INSTANTLY
Tap the mic button when you hear live music. Our audio fingerprinting technology identifies the song in seconds, giving you the title, artist, album, and a direct link to listen on Spotify. Works with both recorded tracks and live performances.

FIND CONCERTS & EVENTS
Browse upcoming concerts and events happening near you. Filter by distance, search for your favorite artists, and see what's playing tonight. Powered by Ticketmaster, so you get real event data with pricing, venue details, and direct ticket links.

CHECK IN AT SHOWS
At a concert? Check in to mark your attendance and share the experience. Build a history of every show you've attended and every song you've discovered live.

LIVE MUSIC MAP
Explore a crowd-sourced map of music discoveries happening in real time. See where songs are being recognized, discover what's playing around the city, and find the live music hotspots near you.

YOUR MUSIC HISTORY
Every song you recognize and every show you attend is saved to your personal profile. Look back on your live music journey anytime.

KEY FEATURES
- Audio fingerprinting song recognition
- Nearby concert and event discovery
- Show check-ins with personal history
- Real-time live music discovery map
- Spotify integration for instant listening
- Ticketmaster integration for tickets
- Location-based event search with radius filter
- Beautiful dark interface designed for venues

Whether you're at a festival, a jazz club, or hearing a street performer, Live Groove Finder helps you capture the moment and find your next show.
