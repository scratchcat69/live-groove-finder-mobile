Privacy Policy

Effective date: February 26, 2026

Live Groove Finder ("we," "our," or "us") operates the Live Groove Finder mobile application and website (collectively, the "Service"). This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our Service.

1. Information We Collect

Account Information

  • Email address and password (for account creation and authentication)
  • Username and profile avatar (optional, set by you)

Audio Data

  • Short audio recordings (up to 10 seconds) captured when you use the song recognition feature
  • Audio is converted to a fingerprint and sent to our recognition service for matching. We do not store raw audio recordings after processing.

Location Data

  • Approximate location (latitude/longitude) when you search for nearby events or check in at a venue
  • Location is only collected with your explicit permission and is used to find events and venues near you

Discovery History

  • Song titles, artists, albums, and metadata from successful recognitions
  • Timestamps and optional location associated with each discovery

Event Check-ins

  • When you check in at events, we store the event details and your user ID

Payment Information

  • If you subscribe to a paid plan, payment processing is handled entirely by Stripe. We do not store your credit card number or banking details. We receive only a confirmation of your subscription status.

Device and Usage Data

  • Push notification tokens (if you enable notifications)
  • General usage data such as features accessed and app interactions

2. How We Use Your Information

  • To provide song recognition and display your discovery history
  • To show events, venues, and concerts near your location
  • To manage your account, profile, and subscription
  • To send push notifications you have opted into (e.g., nearby events, artist updates)
  • To improve and maintain the Service
  • To comply with legal obligations

We implement reasonable technical and organizational measures to protect your information, including:

No method of transmission over the internet is 100% secure. While we strive to protect your data, we cannot guarantee absolute security.
